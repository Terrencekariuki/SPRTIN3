SAFETY

At a construction site,there are various safety measures that need to be put in place.This measures are put in place and need to be implemented inorder to avoid fatal accidents and to ensure the operations run smoothly on site.

Some of this rules are:

  • Hard hats: Protect against head injuries from falling objects.
  • Safety glasses/goggles: Shield eyes from debris and chemicals.
  • Steel-toed boots: Guard feet against heavy objects.
  • High-visibility vests: Enhance visibility, especially in areas with moving equipment.
